# FounderOS Overview

FounderOS is an AI-powered Executive Operating System designed for startups. It gives founders a coordinated team of specialized AI executives for strategy, finance, hiring, growth, legal work, operations, and fundraising.

FounderOS combines multi-agent AI, a RAG knowledge base, persistent startup memory, workflow automation, decision intelligence, and an executive dashboard. Critical actions remain subject to human approval.

Its mission is to reduce founder workload by automating complete, coordinated business workflows instead of isolated tasks.
