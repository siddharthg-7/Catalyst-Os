# Smart Hiring

Smart Hiring uses AI to streamline recruitment. It supports resume analysis, candidate scoring, skill matching, interview recommendations, and hiring workflow automation.

## Backend Engineer Hiring Workflow

For a backend engineering request, the Head of Talent analyzes the hiring need and asks the CFO to validate affordability. The workflow then defines the role, generates a job description, evaluates applicants, ranks candidates, produces structured reviews and hiring scores, drafts outreach, recommends interviews, and requests founder approval before external action.

The hiring recommendation should consider engineering skills, startup experience, compensation fit, available budget, and interview evidence. Missing evidence must be reported instead of guessed.
